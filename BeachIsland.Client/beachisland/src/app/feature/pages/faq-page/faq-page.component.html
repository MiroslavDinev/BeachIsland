Frequently Asked Questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Beach Island (part of Dream Island Group) is only required if you will be selling or buying items.

This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I submit my own item to sell?

In order to sell item you should become a partner. All items you add as a partner undergo a thorough check from our administration.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

Please note this have be discussed with our team for the specific listing but in most of the cases we use USD, GBP or BTC.

Who cen sell items?

Any registered partner can post on Beach Island. The listing will appear on the site after administrator approval.

I want to sell my items - what are the steps?
The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account by clicking on the link we have sent to your email
  • Go to the Become partner section and register as a partner
  • Add your offer
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 72 hours.
How much do I get from each sale?

Here at Beach Island, we discuss the amount individually with every partner depending on item price, location, etc.

Why sell my items here?
There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • We offer a great rate for your items.
  • Fast response/approval times for your offer. And if it gets rejected, there is another iteration. We have eliminated this, and made the process very fast. It only takes up to 72 hours for an offer to be approved/reviewed.
  • We are an exclusive marketplace. There are almost no sites like Dream Island in the world selling so rare and unique items.
When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account.

I want to buy an item - what are the steps?

Buying an item on Beach Island is really simple. Each offer has a book button.

After pressing it you have to fill a form and an administrator will reach you within 72 hrs for further actions.

Once the transaction is complete, you gain full access to the purchased product.

Are all listed items available?

Each item in Dream Island is available. In rare occasions for some of the items we already have received a query and this has to be resolved first.
